Top Attractions in Banaras

1. Ganges River

Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on the sacred Ganga River and witness the city’s spiritual life unfold at sunrise.

2. Sarnath

Visit the ancient Dhamek Stupa, where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon after enlightenment.

3. Dashashwamedh Ghat

Famous for its grand evening Ganga Aarti, this ghat glows with lights and chants each night by the river.

4. Assi Ghat

A calm riverside spot popular for yoga, sunrise views, and connecting with local culture.

5. Ramnagar Fort

This 17th-century fort showcases royal artifacts, vintage cars, and panoramic views of the Ganges.
