Top Attractions in Banaras

Ganges River

Take a peaceful boat ride on the holy Ganga and enjoy the sunrise view. (About 0.8 km from Hotel)

Sarnath

Visit the place where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon. A calm and spiritual spot. (Around 10 km from Hotel)

Dashashwamedh Ghat

Watch the famous Ganga Aarti in the evening with lights and chants by the river. (About 0.9 km from Hotel)

Assi Ghat

A peaceful ghat known for yoga, morning vibes, and great food nearby. (Around 1.2 km from Hotel)

Ramnagar Fort

Explore the old royal fort with vintage cars and a museum. (Around 5 km from Hotel)
